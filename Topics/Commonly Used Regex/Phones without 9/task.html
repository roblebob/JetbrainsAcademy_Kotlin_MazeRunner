Phones without 9

Write a program that searches a text for all phone numbers that do not contain the digit 9. Numbers can be in one of these formats: XXX-XXX-XXXX, (XXX)-XXX-XXXX, (XXX)XXXXXXX, and XXXXXXXXXX.

Print the search results one per line.


Sample Input:
Phone 1: 123-123-1234. Phone 2: 9991234567. Phone 3: (887)6523456. Phone 4: (988)-745-9821

Sample Output:
123-123-1234
(887)6523456



Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 8 seconds
